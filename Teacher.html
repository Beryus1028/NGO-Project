Teachers' professional development



About project

We help to develop the skills needed to make a difference.

At the root of the issue is the lack of resources available

to prospective teachers, including basic equipment as well

as trainers themselves.We aim to provide learning supplies

and incentivize teacher training experts.

Starting point

Engaging with educational experts, we uncovered what the most fundamental tools are for trainee teachers. This included how much time was required for training.

Support from local communities

Finding out the main enduring barriers to education, as well as the most essential requirements to childhood development in Nigeria, was at the core of our mission here.

End result

By listening, engaging, and understanding exactly what was needed to empower educators in Nigeria, we were able to equip 48 teachers with the necessary skills to use in the classroom.
