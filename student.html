HELPING HANDS

Schools in Ikorodu, Lagos

About the project

Our first project was a new school in Ikorodu, Lagos for children under 15 years old. In addition to the long-lasting impact of the catastrophic pandemic in 2020, existing schools are severely lacking in basic supplies of educational tools like stationary, mathematical equipment, and classroom equipment.

Starting point

The first step to understand the scope of the project was to learn what shaped a child's learning experience in Ikorodu and to find out how we could make improvements.

Support from local communities

Location, scale, and accessibility was absolutely crucial to this project. We wanted to make the biggest but most realistic impact to the educational landscape in Ikorodu Lagos.

End result

Contextualizing the needs of the school children, their parents, and their educators helped us to construct a well-equipped, bright, and accessible place of learning.
